The Members of the Order

Through the course of its history the Order has always been headed by an Annalist. The Annalist is the one who keeps the historic records of the Order, and ensures they remain true to the records of the past. Assisting the Annalist in this goal are all manner of elevated Knights that range the spectrum of actions and activities. The core of the Order has, and always will be, the Knights of the Grave that serve the Light foremost. Frequently the Order has brought in those lacking in Faith and Vigor in the form of Penitents. Many such Penitents have gone on to redeem themselves in the Light’s grace through service. Those that survive such redemption are fully welcomed into the familial nature of the Order. The current members of the Order and their ranks are listed below.

Annalist

Charles Schacktorn

The man is as grim and mysterious as they come. The Annalist of the Order has lead for many years, but his strange nature and even stranger habits remain the talk of the Order. Rarely is his face revealed from the masked helm so often worn by the man. His very aura is a strange one of conflicting magics. Both the Light and the Arcane can be felt in him strongly. Whatever his purpose, though... He leads the Order as their heart and their history alike. His guiding hand firm as it directs those new Knights under him to their place within the Order.


Sacristan

Radhan Ar'nareth

Dredged from only the dustiest topographies of time, Radhan occupies a position as the Order’s quartermaster, relic-duster, paper-pusher, and whatever else the Annalist abstains from taking care of. As a Quel’dorei of venerable age, it’s often questionable whether or not a given artifact is older than his long-trodden lifespan, earning him the often-dubbed epithet, ‘Relic’. When not on the campaign trail, he is typically lost in the Order’s labyrinthian vaults.



Custodes

Carlisle Lanris

Dedication. A word that defines the huntsman. Some would use the term zeal and they would not be mistaken. It means the same thing after all. But with zeal comes a certain level of fanaticism. Calling Carlisle Lanris a fanatic almost seems cheap. He is a pious man, true. But he is of a tempered faith. Born and bred, raised, trained under the principles of the Church of the Holy Light. The huntsman is a testament to the Lights doctrine. He is Inexorable in his drive to cleanse the world of the impure. He shall never waver.

Ruskin Blackemore

There's a fleeting glint of mischief in the eyes of the man before you. His mannerisms are a bit rough and he often seems to have a crooked grin on his face. Ruskin Blackemore usually seems at ease, even in dire situations. He's quick to quip, and often raises the hackles of others with his somewhat carefree attitude. Despite this outer calm, he cares for his Brothers and Sisters, and often worries for their safety.

Siphiah Dawnbane

Known formally by her Silver Hand title—The Justiciar—Siphiah serves as a Grave Warden of the Order, and a stalwart presence on its front line. Few could doubt her effectiveness on the field, which is best described as a mixture of brute force and blunt diplomacy. Her history is a complex matter: a case of outcast nobility, blurred allegiances, and bad blood. The shadows of wars past seem to darken her step, and cast her figure in a fair amount of mystery.

Edgar Holden

A young and impressionable man with a keen eye for the right details. Though the Light may give and take his odd luck as it pleases, it is never uncertain to be the latest spark of his interest and desire to know enough.

Grave Wardens

Anna Forskare

A devoted servant of the Light. Few match her zeal and fervor when she gets started. She ensures loyalty and cause in the Order. Her watchful eyes are always there, always lingering. Her senses attuned to slip-ups in those Brothers and Sisters she watches. Her time in the Order is long, and her history with the Annalist even longer still. There is no question to her deference to him, or to the purpose of the Order. There is mystery between the two of them that cannot exactly be pinned down.

Amilia Tervander

What is there to say of Amilia Tervander? One so rash, yet steadfast in her faith. Quiet, yet dangerously boisterous. Clever and rough-hewn, Amilia is an ardent ally for her compatriots among the Order of Ash. Despite her youth compared to most of her companions -- there is a wisdom beyond her years buried behind an icy countenance. A lifetime of struggle has forged a woman steeped in deep paranoia -- a fuel to burn in the relentless, reckless pursuit of her goals. Light help you if your demise is among them.

Beckwith Valdis

Once a Stormwind army medic and a Silver Hand executioner, now a practicing physician among the ranks of the Order. Adhering to traditional medicine with a touch of Light healing, she will always put the patient first, even if that means taking the blow in their stead. Preventative medicine is after all, in her opinion, the best medicine. She is available 24/7 for free medical consultations, mental health counseling, or stoking your gambling addiction with a game of cards. Health insurance not necessary.

Lauren Skylar

Despite her years of experience on the frontlines of Azeroth's crises, Lauren is not unlike a duck on the water. Calm and collected on the surface. Paddling for dear life just underneath. A reliable, stalwart defender of those she finds worthy, Lauren is a staunch ally -- and relentless opponent. Haunted by the sins of her past, Lauren finds among the Order that which she does not believe she deserves - family -- and refuge.

Illionna Sinlefel

Compassion Incarnate, but a fire that burnt to the core if need be, the Medic evened the scales. She walked in harmony with the Light, devout throughout her days, some say it coursed through her veins like rushing rivers. The essence of life was precious to her and she would always lend a hand to those in need, comrades and strangers alike. Formerly of the Silver Hand and a traveling Medic, her skills in mending even the worst wounds with the light were superb. Not to say she relied on it for everything, traditional medicine had its place as well. Dare say she was a bit of an alchemist too. She landed her way in the Order in quite dark moments, but she rises in the Light to aid all those she now holds dear. Her origin speaks of noble Gilnean roots, but a mystery looms within her and her heritage.

Molly Sykes

Having grown up in the clutches of the Scarlet Crusade, Molly Sykes has long been questioning her faith and her place in the world since she left it behind in Northrend. Through the Order she has found a new purpose and a sense of belonging, as well as hope for a life she had once thought to be over with the contraction of the worgen curse.

Sabine Graham

Ruled by the Light, Sabine is an embodiment of its strength - powerful enough to drown you, soft enough to cleanse you, and deep enough to save you. A wise woman who knows her limits - she has none. Ajax Stravenor

Ajax isn’t super tall, coming in at 6’ and his body, while not lithe, is still on the thinner side with hard ‘whipcord’ musculature. He’s broadly built but not quite at what someone would call a healthy weight. He has chestnut brown hair, though that’s only on his face. He’s not naturally bald, but instead chooses to use a razor to remove all hair from the top of his head. His skin is pale due to spending a lot of time indoors reading/researching/what have you, and the time spent outdoors is usually at dusk or night to take enemies by surprise. He has very aquiline features with a prominent nose bridge that steeply slopes down to the tip. Almost like a woodblock shoved in his face at a slight angle. He has piercing silver eyes which are the result of the accident that severed him from the light during his time as a paladin. He usually has a very severe, humorless look on his face but that’s just resting bitch face for the most part. He looks to be early 30s despite his age of 56, and has noticed he hasn’t been aging properly for quite a few years (as he confessed to Carlisle during his introductory interview when first coming to the order.) He wears travel clothing with bits of leather armor protecting more vital bits of his body, and always wears his normal hat which he seems to be very attached to, and very frequently hides his eyes from casual eye contact unless in combat, when it’s pushed back a bit to allow him a better field of view. He uses a spear, rifle, and alchemical orbs (usually fire, but sometimes more caustic or acidic mixtures). His fighting style with the spear is more ‘wushu’ with sweeping slashes and haft attacks rather than the jabs and short slashes normally associated with spear combat.

The Oath of the Grave

“I swear to uphold the Oath of the Grave. I swear that Death shall not be taken lightly, ending a life before it is right will prevent that life from getting its fair judgement. I swear that Death is a transition to another existence, and the physical forms of the dead should be properly interred and laid to rest- be that in the earth or by blessed flame. All remains are sacrosanct and demand respect. I hold faith that The undead are a blight and a perversion of the natural order. They must be eradicated for the greater good of all living beings. Those that manipulate the powers of undeath will meet a swift and just end. I promise that there should be no reason to fear death, as it is simply a transition to a realm appropriate for one’s true self. I abide that relics of great power shall not fall into the hands of the unjust, and that those creatures of ill intent that seek to harm the balance must be met with swift and vigorous punishment. I see to the fact that should I come into possession of such Light blessed items, or items cursed by ruinous powers, that I must treat them with sacrosanct respect to ensure their sanctity and protection no matter the cause. Destruction of ancient relics is a grave offence, and I swear to uphold the protection of such items no matter the cause. I swear all these things upon my Oath as a Knight of the Grave, and that my Oath binds me to such a cause until Death itself decrees that I am to be taken into its embrace. I do all of this with no reservations in my heart, and no worry in my mind. May this Oath keep until my dying breath. “

Ready to Serve the Light?

The Order of Ash is always looking for new characters to take part in our guild events and rp. Light-based characters, zealots, or characters that are new to the faith are all welcome.

From shining paladins to keen-eyed huntsmen, there's a place for all sorts within the Order's ranks. Healers, arcane mages, scouts, and many other varieties of characters can grow in the twice a week rp events. Past themes include hunting down persons of interest, holy and unholy relics, and pursuing a variety of death knights after the events of the shattered sky. Reach out today to learn more!
